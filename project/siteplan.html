Site Name

Selfless CE Uganda Student Support Portal

This platform provides a central hub for students and tutors to access program information, track progress, and manage academic tasks efficiently.

Site Purpose

The website’s main goal is to improve communication and organization by providing:

  • Program details and schedules for students and tutors
  • GPA tracking and attendance guidelines
  • Task management and weekly progress tracking

Scenarios

  • How can a student track weekly academic progress and submit tasks on time?
  • Where can tutors view their schedule and track student attendance?

Color Schema

  • Black (#000000): Primary background and layout
  • Maroon (#800000): Headers, buttons, and accents
  • White (#ffffff): Text and content areas

Typography

Body Font: Roboto — clean and readable for general content.
Heading Font: Georgia — professional, clear headings.
